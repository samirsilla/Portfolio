The Challenge

When Pixodime approached me, some of their biggest website priorities were improving user experience and search engine ranking, having an easy-to-use CMS for rapid content creation, and creating a mobile-friendly design to accommodate the 70+% of customers who preferred viewing the site on a mobile device.

The Approach

With these goals in mind, I took out my sketch pad and dug in. Starting with a fully mobile-optimized site that can be viewed on any device, my main focus was to implement a seameless user experience. This included a sticky navigation bar that invites users to get a taste of other pages on the website. In addition, I went with a design that represents a tech-savvy start-up with flair in a modern and innovative way.

The Solution

Re-engineered Pixodime Website

It started with an amazing start-up team, who worked tirelessly to envision a beautiful final product. We ended with a minimal site design that puts emphasis on user experience without sacrificing stunning visuals. Visitors of the site will not want to leave once they have arrived.
